Danielle Wu

I’m a former human resources professional turned UX/UI student. Although HR gave me the satisfaction of helping others, I always felt a calling to do something more creative. Over the years, I volunteered to design announcements for events and company newsletters. The joy I experienced from creating something that was both useful and visually pleasing made me realize I needed to incorporate design and creativity into my work. Enter UX/UI design! This field would allow me to utilize my HR background to solve design problems in an empathetic, yet process-driven way.
